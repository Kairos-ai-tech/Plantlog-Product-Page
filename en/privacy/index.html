i.Introduction

Kairos.ai ("we," "us") operates the PlantLog mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to handling your personal data responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, who we share it with, how long we keep it, and how you can exercise your rights.

TL;DR We only collect data necessary to run PlantLog — your account, plant records, photos, and the FCM token used for push notifications. We do not sell your data, do not use it for advertising behavioral tracking, and do not share it with unnecessary third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time.

ii.What We Collect

Account data

  • Your email address (obtained through Sign in with Apple or Google; the former may be Apple's private relay address).
  • Your chosen display name (optional).
  • Account creation date, last login time, preferred language.

Plant & observation content

  • Plant details you add: name, species, location tag, acquired date, notes.
  • Observation events: watering, fertilizing, repotting, pruning, issues, milestones, with associated measurements (height, new / fallen leaves, soil moisture, 1–5 health score, mood tag).
  • Photos you upload (up to 4 per observation).

Device & technical data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token — bound to your device and used to deliver reminders.
  • Device model, iOS version, App version, language and time-zone settings.
  • Crash reports and basic diagnostic logs (can be disabled via iOS system analytics sharing).

Subscription data

  • StoreKit 2 transaction receipts, original transaction IDs, product identifiers (monthly / yearly), renewal and state-change events.
  • We do not collect or store your credit card or payment information — payment is handled entirely by Apple.

What we do NOT collect

  • Precise location (GPS coordinates) — we only use location tags you type yourself (e.g., "Living room," "Balcony").
  • Contacts, calendar, health, financial data, or advertising identifiers (IDFA).
  • Your Apple or Google passwords — we only receive the provider tokens returned by the Sign-In SDKs.

iii.How We Use It

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  1. Provide the service: sync your plants and observations, schedule reminders, process subscriptions.
  2. Deliver AI features: transmit photos you explicitly submit to the identification and diagnosis service (see §v).
  3. Push notifications: deliver FCM → APNs pushes at your scheduled times (see §vi).
  4. Improve the product: analyze usage patterns in anonymized or aggregated form to find bugs and performance issues.
  5. Legal compliance: meet legal obligations, comply with lawful court orders or regulatory requests.

Legal bases under GDPR (Art. 6) for EU users: performance of contract (providing the App), your consent (AI processing, push notifications), our legitimate interests (debugging, security), and legal obligations.

For user authentication, push delivery, and server data storage. Data received includes email, FCM token, and your plant and observation records.

What we don't do We do not sell your personal data. We do not use it for cross-site tracking or behavioral ad targeting. We do not integrate any advertising SDKs.

v.Photos & AI

Photos are central to the App: growth, disease, and species ID all rely on images you capture. How we handle them:

  • Storage: your photos are stored encrypted on Google Cloud Storage, tied to your account.
  • AI transmission: only when you actively tap "Identify" or "Diagnose" is a single photo sent to Plant.id. Automatic backup or browsing does not trigger any AI calls.
  • AI response retention: identification results (species candidates, confidence, diagnoses) are stored in your account for future reference.
  • Model training: we do not use your photos to train any AI model. Plant.id processes received images per its own policy.

vi.Push Notifications

On first launch, we request push authorization. If you agree, a device-bound FCM token is generated and stored on our server.

Push messages are generated by our daily scheduler and contain only the reminder content you configured (e.g., "Felix needs watering") plus the deep-link parameters required by the App .

You can disable push anytime at iOS Settings → Notifications → PlantLog. Disabling push does not affect other App features, but you will no longer receive reminders.

vii.Data Retention

  • While your account exists: your plants, observations, photos, and subscription state are retained for the life of your account.
  • Account deletion: you can delete your account in-app (per Apple's requirements). Deletion cascades:
    • your plants, observations, photos
    • reminders and FCM device records
    • personally identifiable fields from subscription / transaction records
    Cascade deletion typically completes within 72 hours; some backup copies may be purged from backup tapes within 30 days.
  • Legal obligations: where required (tax, AML), we may retain anonymized transaction records until the statutory retention period ends.
  • Crash reports: Firebase crash logs are retained for up to 90 days.

viii.Your Rights

Under applicable law (including GDPR, CCPA, and Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act), you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Portability: via the in-app "Data export" feature or , receive a full JSON dump.
  • Rectification: correct your personal data in-app.
  • Erasure: delete your account anytime ("Settings → Account → Delete account").
  • Object / restrict processing: object to specific processing or request restriction.
  • Withdraw consent: disable push, opt out of AI features, or sign out.
  • Lodge a complaint: EU residents may file with their national data-protection authority; Taiwan residents may file with the Personal Data Protection Committee.

To exercise any of these rights, email kairos.ai.tech@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.

ix.Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Transport-layer encryption (TLS 1.3)
  • Encryption at rest (Google Cloud's native AES-256)
  • Least-privilege access controls; all server access is audit-logged
  • Regular security scanning of dependencies

No network transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, however. In the event of a data breach likely to affect you, we will notify you and the relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by law.

x.Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you learn that a child has provided us data, contact kairos.ai.tech@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

xi.International Data Transfers

Kairos.ai is based in Taiwan. Some of the services we use (Firebase, Plant.id, etc.) may process or store data in other jurisdictions, including the United States and the European Union. For transfers from the EU, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved transfer mechanisms.

xii.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced by updating the "Effective" date on this page and notifying you within the App. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
